Welcome to We Care For Dogs!

What are dogs being treated in our country?

In our country, everyday we see so many dogs. Some pet dogs and some stray dogs. But how many of these dogs are treated with care and condition. Every now and then, we hear about incidents in which dogs are being hurt or even killed. This is very terrible. We Care For Dogs wishes to bring a change in this area.

What we do?

At We Care For Dogs, we look after dogs that do not have a home. We help these dogs to find a home. Not only that we also feed stray dogs and make sure they are not being hurt by anyone. This organization was started 5 years ago and since then we are working towards our mission everyday. But we cannot do this alone we need your support.

What you can do?

You can help us bring the change in the world of dogs. You can help us in three ways:

1. You can donate any amount of money to help these dogs.

2. You can adopt one of these dogs and give it a home.

3. You can volunteer at our organization and strengthen us to help them better.
